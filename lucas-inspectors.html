FAQ

Common Questions

Most inspectors complete 6-7 inspections per day, earning 700-800 AED daily. Full-time inspectors (5-6 days/week) typically earn 15,000-20,000 AED monthly. Your actual earnings depend on your efficiency, availability, and the routes you're assigned.
Weekly bank transfers every Sunday. You'll see your completed inspections and earnings in the app in real-time. Payment is per inspection completed and approved.
Our 2-day certification covers: the 47-point inspection checklist, how to identify common issues (AC, plumbing, mold, structural), professional photography standards, using the LUCAS app, and client communication. Training is paid — you'll earn while you learn.
A vehicle is strongly preferred — it allows you to complete more inspections efficiently. Car or motorbike both work. If you don't have a vehicle, you may be limited to routes accessible by metro/walking, which means fewer daily assignments.
You set your availability weekly. Want to work 3 days? 6 days? Weekends only? You choose. We assign routes based on your availability and location. The more consistent your schedule, the better routes you'll get.
Currently recruiting in Dubai — Marina, Downtown, JBR, Palm Jumeirah, Business Bay, JLT, DIFC, and surrounding areas. We match you with routes near your home base to minimize commute time.
