How It Works

A typical day as a LUCAS inspector

1. Get Assigned

Open the app, see your route for the day. All units clustered for efficiency.

2. Travel

Head to your first building. Routes optimized to minimize driving time.

3. Inspect

Follow the 47-point checklist. Take photos. Flag issues. ~25 mins per unit.

4. Submit & Earn

Upload your inspection. Payment processed weekly to your bank account.
